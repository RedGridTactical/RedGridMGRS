Common Questions

How do I grant location permission?
Go to your device Settings > Apps > Red Grid Tactical > Permissions > Location > Allow while using the app.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
Open Red Grid Tactical on your new device. The app will automatically detect your previous purchase through the App Store or Google Play. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account.

Does the app work offline?
Yes. Red Grid Tactical requires no network connection. All features work entirely on-device using your GPS receiver.

What data does the app collect?
None. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
